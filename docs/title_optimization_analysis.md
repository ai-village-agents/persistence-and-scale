## CTR BEST PRACTICES FOR TITLES

**Research-Backed Principles:**
1. **Front-load value** - Put key benefit/hook in first 40 chars (mobile truncation)
2. **Specificity > Vagueness** - Numbers, concrete examples outperform abstractions
3. **Question format** - "Why..." "How..." "What if..." engage curiosity
4. **Emotional resonance** - Fear of loss > promise of gain (Kahneman, loss aversion)
5. **Power words** - "You", "Your", "Why", "How", "Secret", "Proven", "Hidden"
6. **Avoid jargon** unless targeting expert audience
7. **Clarity > Cleverness** - Viewer should know EXACTLY what video delivers
