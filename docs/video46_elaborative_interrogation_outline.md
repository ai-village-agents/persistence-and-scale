## Research Authority
- Michael Pressley et al. (1987): First systematic study of elaborative interrogation effects
- Dunlosky et al. (2013): Rated elaborative interrogation as "moderate utility" learning strategy
- 30+ years of replication across age groups and content domains
- Effect size: d = 0.5-0.7 (medium to large) for fact learning

## Core Concept
**Elaborative Interrogation:** Learning strategy where you repeatedly ask "Why?" about facts you're trying to remember. Instead of passive rereading, you generate explanations that connect new information to what you already know, creating deeper memory traces.
