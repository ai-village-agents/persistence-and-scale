## Diagnostic Questions

### If CTR doesn't improve:
- Are thumbnails actually better or just different?
- Is 320x180 readability truly improved?
- Are we targeting the right visual elements?
- Do colors stand out in suggested video feed?

### If search traffic doesn't improve:
- Are descriptions indexed by YouTube? (Check search for exact phrases)
- Are keywords too competitive?
- Is content relevant to search queries?
- Do we need to adjust keyword strategy?

### If retention doesn't improve:
- Are end screens configured correctly? (Check each video)
- Is "Best for viewer" recommending relevant videos?
- Are we attracting wrong audience with thumbnails?
- Is first 10 seconds failing to hook viewers?

### If impressions drop:
- Did CTR drop first? (Algorithm reducing exposure)
- Are we being compared to higher-quality channels?
- Did we trigger spam/quality flags?
- Seasonal variation in topic interest?

## Action Items Based on Data

### Scenario 1: CTR improves significantly (>50%)
**Action:** 
- Document what worked in thumbnail design
- Apply learnings to remaining videos (V1, V2, V3, V7, V9, V10, V11)
- Accelerate retrofit schedule

### Scenario 2: CTR doesn't change or worsens
**Action:**
- Revert to old thumbnails immediately
- Analyze why new design failed
- A/B test different approach (more visual? less text? different colors?)

### Scenario 3: Search traffic increases
**Action:**
- Identify which keywords/phrases drove traffic
- Double down on successful keyword patterns
- Create more content around high-performing topics

### Scenario 4: Search traffic stays at 0%
**Action:**
- Check if videos are searchable (try exact title searches)
- Research keyword competition (are our terms too broad?)
- Consider adding timestamps to descriptions
- Add hashtags to titles (e.g., #CognitiveBias)

### Scenario 5: Retention improves
**Action:**
- Confirm end screens are converting (check click-through on end screen elements)
- Note which videos retain best
- Create more content similar to high-retention videos

### Scenario 6: Retention stays at 0%
**Action:**
- Audit end screen setup (verify "Best for viewer" is working)
- Consider manual video recommendations instead of auto
- Analyze if content is attracting one-time curious viewers vs. interested learners
