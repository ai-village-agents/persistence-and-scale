## VISUAL ELEMENTS NEEDED

### 1. Inverted U Curve (Core Graphic)
- Clean parabola shape
- X-axis: "Effort" (low → high)
- Y-axis: "Performance" (low → high)
- Peak clearly marked
- Zones labeled: "Under-aroused", "Optimal Zone", "Over-aroused"
- Color gradient or shading to show zones
- Professional, clean design

### 2. Example Animations
**Thread & Needle:**
- Left side: Relaxed hand, steady, successful threading
- Right side: Tense hand, shaking, failing
- Split screen or before/after

**Remembering:**
- Person thinking
- Thought bubble with question mark
- Strain visual (tension)
- Then lightbulb moment when relaxed

**Falling Asleep:**
- Person in bed
- Clock showing late time
- Thought bubbles: "Must sleep!" → tension
- Then letting go → peaceful sleep

### 3. Metaphor: Grip Transformation
- Tight fist (tense, white knuckles)
- Gradual opening
- Relaxed open hand
- Show transformation as visual metaphor

### 4. Checklist Visual
- Three steps clearly numbered
- Brief text only:
  1. "Notice: tension, strain?"
  2. "Ask: What if easier?"
  3. "Try: 80% not 110%"
- Visual hierarchy
- Icons or symbols to support each step

### 5. Brain/Body Diagram
- Simple representation
- Three zones marked
- Highlight "over-aroused" zone
- Arrows or indicators showing tension, narrow attention, reduced control

## COLOR PALETTE

**Theme:** Calming (topic is about reducing strain)

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep blue: #2C5F8D (calm, professional)
- Soft teal: #4A9B9B (soothing)
- Warm gray: #6B7280 (neutral)

**Accent Colors:**
- Tension/problem: #D97757 (muted orange-red, not alarming)
- Optimal/solution: #7FB69E (soft green)
- Highlight: #F4D06F (soft yellow)

**Background:**
- Light gray: #F5F5F5 or white
- Black for contrast where needed

**Text:**
- Primary: #1F2937 (near-black)
- Secondary: #6B7280 (gray)
- Labels: High contrast for readability

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Title Font:** Bold, sans-serif, large
**Body Text (minimal):** Clean sans-serif, readable
**Labels:** Clear, high contrast
**Emphasis:** Bold or color, not excessive
